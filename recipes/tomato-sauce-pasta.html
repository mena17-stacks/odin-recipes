Tomato sauce pasta

Description

Simple, easy home-made tomato sauce recipe to go with your spaghetti. It quick and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Previously cooked Spaghetti
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 2 (or follow your heart) minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • .5 tsp salt
  • .5 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbs butter
  • tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup water
  1. Pour oil to a skillet on medium heat.
  2. Add the garlic, basil, red pepper flakes, salt, sugar and butter. Stir to combine.
  3. Let simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Add the tomato paste and water. stir really well. Reduce heat to low.
  5. Simmer for 25 minutes.
  6. Mix with the previously cooked spaghetti and serve.
