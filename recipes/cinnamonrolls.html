Cinnamon Rolls

Description

There's nothing more perfect for autumn than cinnamon rolls with a nice cup of coffee or hot chocolate. When i think of cinnamon rolls, i think of a warm cozy feeling on looking a rainy day. These cinnamon rolls are easy enough for even an amateur baker. They were teh first thing i have ever baked, myself. This recipe gives you the option to make the day before or the same day.

Ingredients

Rolls
  • 2 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 and 1/4 tsp instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tbs unsalted butter
  • 1 large egg
Filling
  • 3 tbs unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tbs ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup granulated or brown sugar
Icing
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 4 tbs milk

Steps

  1. For the dough, whisk the flour, sugar, salt, and yeast together in a large bowl. set aside.
  2. Combine the milk, water and butter together in a heatproof bowl. Heat until the butter is melted and it is warm to touch. Pour into the dry ingredients, add the egg, and stir until it forms a soft dough (or use mixer).
  3. On a lightly floured surface using floured hands, knead the dough for 3 minutes.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover loosely and allow to rest for about 10 minutes.
  5. Next, roll the dough out in a 14x8 inch rectangle.
  6. Spread the softened butter on top. Mix together the cinnamon and sugar and sprinkle on top.
  7. Roll up the dough tightly to make a log. Cut into 10-12 even rolls and arrange in a lightly creased round cake pan.
  8. Tightly cover the rolls with plastic wrap.
  9. Either refrigerate overnight or allow to rise in a warm, draft free environment for 60-90 minutes. If you decide to refrigerate, then take it out and allow it to rest for 10 minutes.
  10. After the rolls have doubled in size, preheat the oven to 375ºF/190ºC. Bake for 25-28 minutes until lightly brown.
  11. Melt the butter. Then in a mixing bowl, combine the butter with powdered sugar, vanilla and milk.
  12. Blend until smooth and pour over the warm rolls. Then serve.
